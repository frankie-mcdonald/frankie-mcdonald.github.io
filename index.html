About me

As an Indigenous 3D animation artist, I’ve spent over five years bringing St’at’imc stories to life through animation, blending creativity and technology to share meaningful stories and educational content. My passion for storytelling and culture has been at the heart of my work, from crafting dynamic character animations to designing intricate environments inspired by Indigenous design.

I’ve built a strong foundation in tools and languages like HTML5, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, React, Node, Express, Git, GitHub, MySQL, Agile Development, Document Object Model(DOM), API's, Web API's, and responsive design.

I am particularly passionate about projects that empower and educate, such as my current work on St'at'imcets with Kaya—an app designed to teach children the St’at’imcets language through interactive games and storytelling. This work reflects my belief in using technology to preserve and revitalize culture while fostering learning and connection.

St'at'imcets with Kaya - 2025

St'at'imc with Kaya is a passion project of mine. Coming from the St'at'imc nation I found that we lacked resources to learn the language. This is an app to learn the language with lessons that feature audio, interactive games, and quizzes. The idea is that users will be able to track their progress and hear the words and be able to practice whenever they want.

  • Landing Page

    Page features:
    -Salmon animation
    -An introduction to Kaya in St'at'imcets and English
    -An invite to learn the language

  • Random Word Generator

    Page features:
    -Random word
    -The word used in a sentence
    -Audio to hear the word/sentence

  • Login/ Dashboard

    Page features:
    -Login to keep track of progress
    -A dashboard with all lessons
    -Tracker to keep track of progress and quizzes

  • St'at'imc Alphabet

    Page features:
    -St'at'imcets Alphabet
    -Each letter had an example word
    -Audio to hear the word and practice

  • Numbers 1-10

    Page features:
    -Numbers 1 - 10
    -Audio to hear the word
    -A video to hear all the numbers in a row

  • Quiz Page

    Page features:
    -Quiz to test users knowledge of the numbers lesson
    -Animated salmon
    -Results page to let users know if they passed the quiz.
    -Option to go back to the lesson, or take the quiz again.
